Story of the day

"I am the link between him and the world. My brother can’t hear or speak. I often sit with him during his classes to make him understand what the teacher teaches. And also let others know what he wants to convey. But most of the time, when I am not able to be with him, he gets bullied or laughed at by other children. One day, he was pushed by a senior. My brother didn’t get offended. When the school got over, he went to the senior and smiled at him. Then he gave him a hug. He made a connection. Nobody taught him about anything like this. It was his own thought."

- Adarsh's Sister
